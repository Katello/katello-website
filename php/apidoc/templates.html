Templates

System templates allow to group content (products, repositories, packages) and handle them in changesets as a unit. They also provide an export consumable by different systems (such as Aeolus Conductor to generate system images).

>>>

GET /api/environments/:environment_id/templates
List system templates for given environment.

Examples

GET /api/environments/2/templates/?name=promotion_test_tpl_227e003
200
