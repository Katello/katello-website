Sync

methods for handeling repositories synchronisation. Repositories can be selecteted individualy by id, by product or by provider

>>>

GET /api/providers/:provider_id/sync
Get status of repo synchronisation for given provider

GET /api/organizations/:organization_id/products/:product_id/sync
Get status of repo synchronisation for given product

GET /api/repositories/:repository_id/sync
Get status of synchronisation for given repository

Examples
